About Me

Hello! I am Samuel Wilberforce, an AWS Cloud Computing specialist with a passion for leveraging technology to tackle one of the most pressing challenges in my community: urbanization and sustainable housing in Ghana.

My journey into the cloud is driven by a firsthand understanding of the rapid growth of Ghanaian cities and the critical need for innovative, scalable solutions. I believe that the power of AWS—its ability to process vast datasets, support IoT systems, and host scalable platforms—is the key to revolutionizing how we approach affordable housing and urban planning.

My goal is to become a Cloud Solutions Architect who builds secure, cost-effective, and resilient cloud infrastructures. I want to empower developers, urban planners, and NGOs to create solutions that significantly improve living conditions and foster economic development.

When I'm not architecting cloud solutions, I'm working as a passionate part-time amateur architect, researching how traditional Ghanaian design can meet modern, sustainable building techniques.
